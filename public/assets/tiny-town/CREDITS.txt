These station/ground sprites are composited from the pack's 16x16 tiles
(tilemap_packed.png, 12 cols, index = row*12 + col):

  ground_grass.png         tile 0   (plain grass)
  ground_grass_flowers.png tile 2   (grass + flowers, variety)
  ground_dirt.png          tile 24  (dirt)
  plot.png   16x16  tile 24 (dirt) + tile 17 (sprout)
  mill.png   16x32  tile 49 (blue roof) over tile 89 (stone wall + door)
  coop.png   16x32  tile 53 (red roof)  over tile 85 (wood wall + door)

Re-bake: download the pack from the Source URL, then run the bake script
against Tilemap/tilemap_packed.png (see git history / .asset-src bake.cjs).
